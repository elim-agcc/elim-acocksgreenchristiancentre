Meet the Team

At Acocks Green Christian Centre we believe that God is the head of the Church and we take our lead from him.

We are an Elim Pentecostal church just off Westley Road in Acocks Green.

We are a community of people who are passionate about following Jesus Christ and committed to loving and serving each other and the community around us.
We warmly invite you to join us on Sundays at 10.30am for lively worship and a relevant Bible based message. We also regularly meet in the week connecting through relationships and seeking to encounter God in our lives.

Acocks Green Christian Centre, an Elim Pentecostal Church. Elim Foursquare Gospel Alliance.
